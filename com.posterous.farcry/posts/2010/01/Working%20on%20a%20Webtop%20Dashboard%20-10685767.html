Working on a Webtop Dashboard

January 27 2010, 4:53 PM  by Geoff Bowers
When you first login into the FarCry webtop you're presented with a fairly simple overview of what the various sections of the webtop are about.

This is a actually placeholder for developers to put in a more interesting overview for a specific FarCry CMS implementation.  But in line with our thinking for the next generation FarCry, we want "placeholders" to be the very best they can be from the start.  That is, still give folks the option to change the Overview but start with something most people will find very useful and a great starting point for customisation.

So I'm working on a simple dash board for FarCry 6.0. The left hand column has content that you own (ie. where your profile has been assigned as the owner) and the right hand column has information that might be of interest.  It's pretty simple at this stage but will have its own webskin you can override easily within your project (or plugins).

Would you find this useful? What else do you think should go in there?
