You have been denied access to this item

January 9 2010, 9:40 PM  by Geoff Bowers
A very cool feature of FarCry is the ability to actually secure views by role.  " You have been denied access to this item" is the standard response from the FarCry "view stack" when a user is attempting to display a webskin for which their role does not have permission. 

FarCry installs with a default list of roles and webskin permissions.  For example, by default the "anonymous" role is allowed to view any webskin that starts display* but little else.  If you are not following the display* prefix standard for public templates then you will need to add the appropriate prefix to your roles under "Admin > Roles" in the webtop admin area.

You can get in and modify these security settings as needed to suit your own particular naming conventions for views.
