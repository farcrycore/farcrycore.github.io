Pretty Dates in the Webtop

January 21 2010, 4:22 PM  by Geoff Bowers
One of the big usability improvements in the webtop for Formality (upcoming 6.0 release) is the introduction of "pretty dates''.  All dates are reformatted to give the contributor a relative time "ago" instead of a full blown date.  This makes it easy to see at a glance just how recent or old something is.  Of course if you need to see the full date-time stamp its there -- just hover over the date till you get a tool tip.

The content admin grids have dates converted -- you can still reorder the columns as you might expect.

And each object overview has the date of the last update clearly shown at all times.
