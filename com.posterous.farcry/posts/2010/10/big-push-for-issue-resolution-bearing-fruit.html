Big Push for Issue Resolution Bearing Fruit

October 19 2010, 12:21 AM  by Geoff Bowers

There's been a massive push internally at Daemon to attack the outstanding issue log (both bugs and enhancements) for FarCry framework in the lead up to the 6.1 release (due by Christmas).

Many thanks to all those folks reporting and documenting issues, and to those fearless committers who've been bug fixing away -- "we love youse all!"

But there's still a ways to go before we'll be all "charlie the unicorn" for 6.1 Mayonaise release. Happily though many of these fixes are going straight into the 6.0.x maintenance branch so you can enjoy the fruits our labours immediately.
