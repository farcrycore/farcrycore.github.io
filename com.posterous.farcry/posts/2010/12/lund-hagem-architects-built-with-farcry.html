Lund Hagem Architects Built With FarCry

December 2 2010, 12:37 AM  by Geoff Bowers

From the other side of the world, our Norwegian friends have put together a beautiful web site for architects Lund Hagem.


FarCry content types and flash remoting componentry built by Jorgen and Odd-Geir's team at IDLmedia. Design and AS3/Flash by Bleed; the digital agency with a penchant for Scandinavian blondes doing yoga naked. 


Don't have Flash? You really are missing out. But hey for those on iOS and similar devices there's also an elegant HTML only solution rendering the same wonderful content. Overall a quality effort.


Enjoy!
