FarCry Dev Camp Podcasts

December 16 2009, 7:50 PM  by Scott Gamble
Melbourne Law School was the location for two of our finest to spend a day discussing all things FarCry. 
Mat Bryant (Lead Author) and Geoff Bowers (self-styled Benevolent Dictator) spent all of 11 November talking to a rabid audience about FarCry's past, present and future. And believe me, the future's so bright, we're all wearing shades around the office.
Edited into seven, delicious, bite-sized chunks, the FarCry Dev Camp podcasts are like a home-cooked meal when you've been traveling the third world for several months.
1. Opening Session - What is it? How does it work?
2. FarCry Roadmap - Where is it going?
3. FarCry 101 - More on how it works.
4. Melbourne Law School case study.
5. 6. 7. Mat and Geoff build a site with audience suggestions. Expect lengthy diversions from questions and answers. Defies easy categorisation.

Here it is: http://www.farcrycore.org/podcast/farcry-dev-camp
