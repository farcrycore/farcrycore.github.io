FarCry DevCamp, Melbourne (11-Nov-2009)

October 20 2009, 6:46 PM  by Geoff Bowers
Daemon is running a one day developer camp for FarCry solution developers in Melbourne on the 11 November 2009 (to coincide with the cf.objective() developers conference on 12-13 November 2009). The DevCamp is “free” and open to all developers interested in the FarCry platform.

We're looking for speakers! If you have a technical session (50mins) or case study (20mins) let us know. Or if you have an absolute-must-have topic you'd like to see covered tell us in the comments.
